Match an invoice

Overview

Automatically check for discrepancies between billed and received goods by matching an invoice. You can perform a two-way match) with an invoice and a PO or a RTFM-Industries > Invoices.

If discrepancies are found within your configured tolerance limits, they are automatically reconciled and the difference is inserted as a balancing line item on the PO. If discrepancies exceed your configured tolerance limits, you are prompted to resolve them manually.

Additional processing options

To request information or record an invoice exception, see the Additional processing options section.

Prerequisites

  • Invoicing setup

  • Invoice in To match status

  • "Invoice matching" permission

Location

You can match an invoice in two locations depending on the type of match you're doing:

Match to a PO (two-way match)

  • Procurement > POs

Match to a receipt (three-way match)

Procurement > Receipts

Match an invoice to a PO

Create an invoice from a PO and check for discrepancies with a 2-way match. The PO must be in Sent status.

Match an invoice to a receipt

Check for discrepancies with a 3-way match between an invoice, PO and receipt.

Additional processing options

When an invoice is in the To match status, a user has the following processing options that appear above the state machine.

Request information

To request additional information regarding an invoice, click the Request info button above the status line. The point of contact user is notified by email as a result.

Provide instructions to the point of contact

You can provide instructions for the point of contact either manually on the invoice or automatically through a configured automatic invoice processing rule.

To manually provide instructions, Edit the invoice, update the "Instructions for point of contact" field and Save. These instructions and the assigned point of contact are saved in the Invoicing processing tab.

Unassign an invoice

To handle an invoice exception found while matching, assign the proper user to the invoice in the Invoice processing tab and click the Unassign button above the status line. The user assigned to the invoice is notified by email and the invoice's status updates to "To assign" for reprocessing.
