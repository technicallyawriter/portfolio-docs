Match a supplier invoice

Overview

Automatically check for discrepancies between billed and received goods by matching a supplier invoice to a purchase order (two-way match) or receipt (three-way match). Once a supplier invoice is matched, its state updates to Matched and a read-only Linked invoice is created in Invoicing > Invoices.

If discrepancies are found within your configured tolerance limits, they are automatically reconciled and the difference is inserted as a balancing line item on the PO. If discrepancies exceed your configured tolerance limits, you are prompted to resolve them manually.

Additional processing options

To request information or record an invoice exception, see the Additional processing options section.

Prerequisites

  • Invoicing setup

  • Supplier invoice in To match state

  • Invoice matching or Invoice entering authorization depending on organization configuration

Location

Depending on where you are in the procure-to-pay process, you can match an invoice in the following locations:

Match to a PO (two-way match)

  • Homepage > To do > Your POs to match

  • Procurement > POs

Match to a receipt (three-way match)

Procurement > Receipts

Match a supplier invoice to a PO

Create a supplier invoice from a PO and check for discrepancies with a 2-way match. PO must be in Sent state.

Match a supplier invoice to a receipt

Check for discrepancies with a 3-way match between a supplier invoice, PO and receipt.

Additional processing options

When a supplier invoice is in the To match state, a user has the following processing options that appear above the state machine.

Request information

To request additional information regarding a supplier invoice click the Request info button above the state machine. The point of contact user is notified by email.

Provide instructions to the point of contact

Note: the supplier invoice must be in To match state to provide instructions to the point of contact.

Any active user can be assigned as a point of contact, and a default point of contact can be configured in General Settings > Invoicing. You can provide instructions for the point of contact either manually on the invoice or automatically through a configured automatic invoice processing rule.

To manually provide instructions, Edit the supplier invoice, type into the "Instructions for point of contact" field and Save. These instructions and the assigned point of contact appear in the Invoicing processing tab.

Unassign an invoice

To handle an invoice exception found while matching, assign the proper user to the supplier invoice in the Invoice processing tab and click the Unassign button above the state machine. The user assigned to the supplier invoice is notified by email and the supplier invoice returns to the To assign state for reprocessing.
